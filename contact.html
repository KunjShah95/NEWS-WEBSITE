Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit a news tip?

You can submit news tips by emailing tips@primenews.com or by using the contact form above. Please include as much relevant information as possible.

How do I subscribe to your newsletter?

You can subscribe to our newsletter by checking the subscription box in our contact form, or by visiting the Subscribe page in the main navigation.

Do you offer advertising opportunities?

Yes, we offer various advertising opportunities including display ads, sponsored content, and newsletter placements. Please contact our advertising team at advertising@primenews.com.

How can I report technical issues with the website?

For technical issues, please email support@primenews.com with details about the problem, including screenshots if possible, and information about your device and browser.
